About

I am currently a student studying computer science. Although I have a variety of software development interests, most of my experience centers around web design. I designed and coded this website from the ground up, and it serves as a sandbox for me to experiment with different web development practices. The website is currently using Node.js Express as a lightweight framework and technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery. I am currently working to move the page to a more robust framework, more specifically .NET MVC. Software development intrigues me for the same reason that I enjoy photography - there's creativity that goes into developing a new solution for a problem.

Photography and videography are both hobbies that I like to pursue in my free time. I am often outside exploring everything that the Upper Peninsula has to offer, and I often take my camera along with me. Some of my shooting is done with film - there's something awesome about being thoughtful about a photo composition and not heavily processing it with tools like Photoshop. I usually can be found shooting landscapes, but I also love taking portraits. My photography is all about capturing those little moments in life. As far as video, my works cover everything from juggling to simple B-roll to short films. I have fun with it. Check out some of my videography and photography!
